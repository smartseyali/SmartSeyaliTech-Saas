Pattikadai - A Brand of Thandatti Foods

Authentic Tradition, Crafted by Grandma's Hands

Pattikadai is an online home for pure, healthy, preservative-free country foods inspired by Grandma Eshwari's traditional cooking. It is our family's love, tradition, and trust packed for yours.

We believe in bringing the authentic taste of Tamil Nadu's countryside directly to your doorstep. Every product is handcrafted using traditional methods passed down through generations, ensuring you get the purest and most nutritious food.

100% Natural

No preservatives, no chemicals. Pure and organic ingredients only.

Handcrafted

Every product is lovingly made by hand using traditional methods.

Pan India Delivery

We deliver the taste of tradition right to your doorstep, anywhere in India.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of Tamil Nadu by bringing authentic, preservative-free country foods to every Indian household. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional cooking and modern convenience.
